Our Legacy Since 1884

For over 138 years, Dabur has been committed to bringing the best of Ayurveda to millions of homes worldwide.

1884

Foundation

Dr. S.K. Burman establishes Dabur in Calcutta with a mission to provide effective Ayurvedic medicines to all.

1910

First Manufacturing Unit

Dabur sets up its first manufacturing plant to scale production of authentic Ayurvedic formulations.

1972

Dabur Research Foundation

Establishment of R&D wing to scientifically validate Ayurvedic formulations and ensure quality.

1998

Global Expansion

Dabur products reach international markets, sharing the wisdom of Ayurveda with the world.

2020

Sustainability Initiatives

Commitment to sustainable sourcing, eco-friendly packaging and community development across all operations.
